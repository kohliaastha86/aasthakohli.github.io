About

I am a seasoned regulatory affairs professional with over 12 years of experience specializing in precision medicine, drug-diagnostic co-development, and medical device regulations. Currently serving as Senior Director of Regulatory Affairs at Caris Life Sciences, I lead regulatory strategy and compliance for global product registrations.

My expertise spans the entire product lifecycle from early development through post-market surveillance, with particular focus on in vitro diagnostics (IVDs), companion diagnostics, and the integration of precision medicine approaches in drug development. I have successfully navigated complex regulatory pathways across multiple jurisdictions including US FDA, EU IVDR, and global markets.

Throughout my career at leading organizations including Gilead Sciences and Novartis Precision Medicine, I have established myself as a strategic leader in drug-diagnostic co-registration, guiding cross-functional teams through IDE submissions, PMA approvals, EU IVDR implementation, and health authority interactions. I am recognized for my ability to translate complex regulatory requirements into actionable strategies that advance precision medicine initiatives.
